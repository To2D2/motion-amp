Amplification Settings

30×
How strongly the filtered signal is added back to the image. Pair high values with some noise smoothing below.
1.20 Hz
The frequency of motion to isolate. ~1–1.7 Hz ≈ resting heart rate, ~0.2–0.4 Hz ≈ breathing.
0.80 Hz
Width of the frequency band around the center frequency that gets amplified.
1.5
Blurs the signal the filter reads (not the image you see) to suppress sensor noise, so higher amplification stays clean instead of grainy.
"Signal only" shows just the detected motion/color change, isolated from the image — useful for confirming a signal is present before dialing in settings.
1.00×
Slows down playback so fast, high-frequency motion is easier to see. Frequency/bandwidth settings still target the video's real-world Hz.
720p
Lower resolution often unlocks a higher frame rate from your camera, which improves amplification accuracy.
